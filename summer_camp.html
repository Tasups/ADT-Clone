Summer Camps

Every summer we hold 2 summer theme camps for ages 3-10. These camps incorporate a themed week with craft, snack, short movies, dancing to the theme music and an end of week performance. In the past our themes have included Princess Camp, Encanto Camp, Under the Sea Camp and Fairy Camp to name a few.
