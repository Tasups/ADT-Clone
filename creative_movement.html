Creative Movement

Our goal is to make sure that each child is nurtured in their natural creativity in a fun and loving atmosphere. The class provides a strong foundation in the fundamentals of ballet. Dancers will also develop strong balance, coordination, and kinesthetic awareness.

Dancers must be potty trained.
