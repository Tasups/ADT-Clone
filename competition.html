Competitive Company

The Dancing Gems

Our Competitive Company is an elite group of dancers ages 7 and up. These dancers train in all styles of dance including Ballet, Tap, Jazz, and Lyrical. They attend multiple Competitions and Conventions throughout the dance season as well as an out-of-town Nationals event every other Summer. Our Competitive Company also Performs in our annual Holiday and Spring Recitals. Our Competitive Company audition every July. Please contact us if you would like more information about auditioning for our Competitive Dance Company.
