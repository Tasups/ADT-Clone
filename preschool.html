Preschool | Ages 4-5

Preschool classes are an age specific, 45 minute class. This class will spend portions of the time working on ballet and tap, giving the children a fantastic base and exposure to both styles of dance. We have found that this “combination” technique helps to keep the kids excited, interested, and focused for the entire 45 minute period.


Preschool 1 (age 4) | Tuesdays 4:45-5:30 or Wednesdays 4:30-5:15

Preschool 2 (age 5) | Mondays 4:15-5:00 or Tuesdays 5:30-6:15
