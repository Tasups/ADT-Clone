Performances

Holiday Shows

Ages 3-5 will perform at the studio for family and friends a short performance incorporating class exercises and a short Nutcrcacker performance.

Ages 6 and up will perform a Holiday recital on stage.

Annual Spring Recital

Dance is a performing art, and we feel it is an important part of our students' dance education to get up on stage and share their love of dance with an audience. All dancers perform in our annual Spring Recital.

Asheville Holiday Parade

Our Competitive Company also participates in the Asheville Holiday Parade, community events and both local and national dance competitions. Please see the Competitive Company page for more information.
