Asheville Dance Theater

Celebrating 44 YEARS of dance!

Teaching dance since 1979, Asheville Dance Theater, Inc. has been privileged to introduce quality dance instruction to children of all ages. We are committed to helping each dancer develop his/her own talents and abilities. Known for our family-like atmosphere, Asheville Dance Theater wants each dancer to experience growth, confidence and success.
